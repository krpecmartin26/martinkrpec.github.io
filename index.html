About Me

I am an Automation Engineer bridging the gap between standard PLC logic and modern HMI web technologies. Specialized in the Siemens ecosystem (TIA Portal, WinCC Unified) with a strong focus on JavaScript integration. I am a flexible troubleshooter, experienced in international on-site commissioning and delivering reliable code under tight deadlines.

Beyond the technical world, I am an explorer at heart. Whether it is riding my motorcycle across Southern Europe, climbing peaks in the Austrian and Italian Alps, or backpacking through Southeast Asia, I constantly seek new perspectives. This passion for travel has taught me how to adapt to different cultures and embrace the unknown—a mindset I bring to every new project, whether it involves mastering a new technology or navigating a complex start-up on the other side of the world.
